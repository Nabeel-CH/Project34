\chapter{Exploratory and Negative-Control Methods} \label{app:negative_controls}

Several wavelet-scattering and CNN/fusion variants were explored during the project but were \emph{not} selected as final methods. They are preserved with full provenance in the appendix notebook \textit{FINAL Step~2.5 --- Extension Methods Appendix} and summarised in Table~\ref{tab:appendix_methods}. They document the breadth of the search and act as negative controls supporting the concise discussion in Section~\ref{sec:ws_alternatives}; none was adopted because it was unstable, superseded, computationally heavy, or did not beat a simpler baseline.
